Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2025

Welcome to Lotto Genius. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide and improve our services:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the application including preferences, settings, and interactions.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and other technical data.
  • Purchase Information: If you make in-app purchases, we process transaction data via Google Play's billing system.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining our application
  • Improving user experience
  • Processing purchases and providing premium features
  • Analytics to understand app usage
  • Troubleshooting issues

3. Third-Party Services

Our app utilizes the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and in-app purchases
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements (free version only)
  • Google Analytics: For understanding app usage patterns

Each of these services collects and processes data according to their own privacy policies.

4. Data Storage

Your lottery number combinations, favorites, and history are stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store this information on our servers.

5. Ads and Premium Version

The free version of our app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. The premium version removes all advertisements. When you purchase the premium version, your purchase information is processed by Google Play's billing system.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not intended for use by children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: support@lottogeniusapp.com
