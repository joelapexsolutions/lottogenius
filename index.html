About Lotto Genius

Lotto Genius is a powerful lottery number generator designed to help you create optimized number combinations for various lottery games worldwide. Our app uses advanced algorithms to generate unique number sets based on your preferences.

With support for over 17 international lottery formats, Lotto Genius is the perfect companion for any lottery enthusiast. Select from popular games like US Powerball, Mega Millions, EuroJackpot, and many more - each pre-configured with the correct format and rules.
